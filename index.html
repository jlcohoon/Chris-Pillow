About Me

I'm Chris: engineer, pilot, space enthusiast, and interminable tinkerer. At 18 I joined the Navy and became a propulsion mechanic on a nuclear submarine. Six years later I went to work on my mechanical engineering degree at Virginia Tech, which I completed in 2015. I make the most of my free time, constantly seeking out new adventures.
